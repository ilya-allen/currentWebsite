Music

I play the trumpet in multiple bands and help with the social media aspect of the Orewa College Music Department.

National Youth Jazz Band

I was extremely grateful to make it into the 2025 National Youth Jazz Band as Trumpet 5. I had such a fun expierence and met some of the greatest jazz players I've ever seen. This band built me lifelong friendships and it makes me sad that I won't be able to return after graduation. Hope to see these lads again another time. THis photo was taken at the NYJC 2025

Junior Jazz Band

Since the start of 2024 I have been conducting the Orewa College Junior Jazz Band. These guys were the closest to a bronze award in the history of Junior Jazz and so far conducting them has been a massive privilige. This photo was taken at the KBB music festival 2024.

Concert Band

The Orewa College Concert Band focuses upon playing classical and interesting pieces. We practice these pieces for a year and then perform them for adjudicators at the KBB music festival and the NZCBA nationals. This photo was taken at the KBB music festival 2024.

Senior Jazz Band

The Orewa College Senior Jazz Band focuses upon playing jazzy and upbeat pieces. We take a big focus upon soloing aswell. We practice these pieces for a year and then perform them for adjudicators at the KBB music festival and the NZCBA nationals. This photo was taken at the KBB music festival 2024.

Social Media

I help run the Orewa College music department social media and give Orewa College a larger online presence. The social media brings the hype and stores the memories of past years.
